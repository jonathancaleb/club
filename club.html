Q Club

Discover the futuristic Club, the venue will be a pioneering space for nightlife and culture, with a full programme of events and collaborations with big-name DJs and highly influential cultural figures planned from launch.

With a stunning sound system from pro audio experts Pioneer, new lighting rigs including vibrant lasers and more. Table service on 14 tables, and a beautiful central dance floor will give guests the ultimate in opulent night-time entertainment.

The pick of the nightlife scene’s contemporary best house and electronic music will be central to Q Club’s operations.
