Experience the thrill of Vegas in the heart of Shoreditch, at London’s latest luxury venue Q.

Our team offers an all-inclusive, sophisticated experience with thrilling games, top-end cocktails and world-class entertainment.

Be guided through blackjack, poker and roulette by experienced croupiers, or head over to collection of beautiful pool tables where pool professionals and trick shot pros share their expertise.

Set over two floors, Q offers three distinct and immersive spaces, each with their own private cloakroom and entrance, from the elegant Q Lounge, to the intimate Q VIP Room, and the futuristic Q Club below.
