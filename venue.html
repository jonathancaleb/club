Our Story

Step into one of London’s most exciting new venues, with upscale pool tables and games throughout, not to mention a cocktail selection at the bar that alone is worth the visit.

Brought to you by the team behind the legendary Kingly Club and McQueen, A is set over two floors just moments from floors.

An elegant lounge occupies the ground floor, while an intimate, futuristic club awaits below, where you’ll find world-class DJs, showgirls and entertainers every night.

Q offers the excitement of pool and the thrill of competitive gaming, all in one luxurious setting, whether you’re on a night out with a group of friends or hosting an event to remember.

What's On at Q

Room 1

A Lounge

Whether it’s a game of pool or a perfectly-mixed cocktail, Q’s Lounge Bar is the place to be, with the refined and classic style of a 1950s Manhattan loft apartment.

The centrepiece here is the trio of beautiful English pool tables, and there are two casino tables for Blackjack and Poker available too. At the bar itself you’ll find a comfortable space to enjoy our selection of champagne, finely-crafted cocktails and much more.

  • Three English pool tables with fast American cloth and two casino tables
  • Activity gaming experts, pool professionals and trick shot pros in attendance
  • Large-format champagnes, wines and spirits, as well as sharing cocktails and an exciting food menu
  • Bottomless brunch on all week
  • 6x3 metre projection screen
  • 220 capacity lounge bar

Private Hire

Experience the thrill of Vegas in the heart of Shoreditch, at London’s latest luxury venue Q.

Our team offers an all-inclusive, sophisticated experience with thrilling games, top-end cocktails, bar food & canape menus and world-class entertainment.

Be guided through blackjack, poker and roulette by experienced croupiers, or head over to our collection of beautiful pool tables where pool professionals and trick shot pros share their expertise.
