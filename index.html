Discover

Experience the thrill of Vegas as you explore Q’s three distinct rooms, each with their own unique design.

Here you’ll find 4 luxury American pool tables, 7 English pool tables and 5 gaming tables, offering favourites such as Black Jack, Poker and Roulette in our lounge, VIP room and intimate club downstairs. Challenge friends and colleagues, enter tournaments and brush up on your game with a series of masterclasses.

Either drop by for a game, or book pool and gaming tables for both small and large groups in advance. We have a number of exciting packages available.

Dress Code - No sportswear, hats or hoodies.
